# Resilience and Growth: TCS in the AI Era

## Overview
This research project examines Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) and its transformation into an AI-first enterprise. The study explores AI adoption across industries, competitor strategies, financial resilience during the COVID-19 period, leadership evolution, and future technology trends.

## Objectives
- Analyze TCS's AI adoption strategy.
- Compare TCS with Infosys, Wipro, and Accenture.
- Study financial resilience during COVID-19.
- Evaluate leadership's role in digital transformation.
- Explore future AI opportunities and challenges.

## Key Areas Covered
- Artificial Intelligence in TCS
- Competitor Analysis
- Financial Analysis
- Leadership Journey
- Marketing Mix (7Ps)
- Future Technology Trends
- AI & Services Transformation Unit

## Skills Demonstrated
- Business Research
- Strategic Analysis
- Financial Analysis
- Statistical Testing (Paired t-Test)
- Digital Transformation Analysis
